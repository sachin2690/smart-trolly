Smart Trolley System

1. What is a Smart Trolley?

A smart trolley, often referred to as a "smart cart" or "intelligent shopping cart," is a modern take on the traditional shopping trolley. It comes equipped with advanced features such as sensors, real-time tracking, and integration with mobile apps to improve the shopping experience.

2. Key Features of a Smart Trolley

Real-time price tracking, built-in payment solutions, personalized recommendations, navigation assistance, and automatic checkout are standout features that make the smart trolley a game-changer for shoppers.

3. Benefits of Smart Trolley Design

Time-saving, enhanced customer experience, reduced checkout time, and increased efficiency for retailers are some of the major benefits offered by smart trolleys.

4. Payment Gateway Integration

Seamless integration of payment gateways allows hassle-free checkout, enabling customers to pay directly through the trolley without waiting in long lines.

5. The Future of Smart Trolleys

AI integration, self-driving capabilities, and sustainability features are shaping the future of smart trolleys, promising a smarter and greener shopping experience.
